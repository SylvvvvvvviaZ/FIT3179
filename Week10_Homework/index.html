Alcohol, Tobacco, and Other Drugs Impacts in Australia

This dashboard presents two key visualizations related to drug and tobacco use in Australia.

Drug-Related Ambulance Attendances

This visualization shows the number of drug-related ambulance attendances by type and jurisdiction from 2015 to 2023. It highlights the various drugs involved and the frequency of ambulance attendances.

Proportion of Population Who Smoke Daily

This visualization displays the proportion of daily smokers as a percentage of the population aged 15 and over, helping us understand smoking trends across different years and regions in Australia.

This dashboard is created using Vega-Lite visualizations. Data sources include the Australian Institute of Health and Welfare reports on drug-related ambulance attendances and smoking habits.
